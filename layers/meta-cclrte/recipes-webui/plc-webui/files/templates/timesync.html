Select your region and synchronisation method. The Pi clock must match your engineering PC for OPC-UA timestamps and CODESYS log correlation to align.

Region & Timezone
Sets the clock display timezone on the Pi. NTP always syncs to UTC internally.
A
Internet NTP
~5 – 20 ms accuracy
Syncs over WiFi to Cloudflare & Google time servers. Requires wlan0 connected to a WiFi network with internet.
Host side: Nothing required.
B
Engineering PC — Local NTP
< 1 ms accuracy over wired LAN
Pi syncs directly to your PC over the CODESYS eth0 link. Works airgapped — no internet needed.
Host side (Windows) — run as Administrator: 
w32tm /config /manualpeerlist:"time.windows.com" /syncfromflags:manual /reliable:YES /update
net stop w32tm && net start w32tm
Then allow UDP port 123 inbound in Windows Defender Firewall.
C
PTP IEEE 1588
< 1 μs accuracy (hardware timestamps)
Hardware-level precision for microsecond CODESYS / OPC-UA timestamp alignment. Needs NIC hardware timestamp support on both ends.
Host side: Install a PTP grandmaster on the 192.168.2.x network (Meinberg LANTIME, or ptp4l on Linux in master mode). Configure your NIC as PTP master before applying.
